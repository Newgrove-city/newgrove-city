Founding Families of New Grove

The Hawthorne Family

The Hawthorne family were among the first settlers of New Grove, arriving in the early 1800s. They played a key role in establishing the city's groves and orchards and contributed to early governance.

The Silverwood Family

The Silverwood family were renowned merchants who helped establish New Grove’s market district. They also funded the construction of the first bridge over the Silver River, connecting neighborhoods.
