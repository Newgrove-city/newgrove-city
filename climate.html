Climate & Location

Newgrove’s privileged location in California rewards its residents with relatively hot temperatures in the summer and not-too-cold temperatures in the winter. With the river crossing through the city, and the sea to the south, the climate keeps a comfortable and regulated weather all year round. Fortunately, this allows our residents to enjoy lively neighborhoods and entertaining gatherings around town even when the weather gets a little chilly!
